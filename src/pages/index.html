Time the market? Or trade over time?

Cryptocurrencies like Bitcoin can experience daily (or even hourly) price volatility. As with any kind of investment, volatility may cause uncertainty, fear of missing out, or fear of participating at all. When prices are fluctuating, how do you know when to buy?

An introduction to dollar-cost averaging - a long-term investment strategy:

Trade Over Time

Dollar Cost Averaging (DCA) is a strategy that divides a total investment into multiple recurring orders, rather than trying to time the market with one large buy.

Long Term Strategy

DCA is a long-term strategy. It can be beneficial for reducing the impact of volatility on your overall trade amount.
